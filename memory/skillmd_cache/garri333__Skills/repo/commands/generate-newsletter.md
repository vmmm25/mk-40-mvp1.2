## Step 4: Write the Newsletter

### Monthly Update Format

```
Subject: [Specific topic or highlight] — [Month] update de PrivaLex

[Nombre],

[One sentence: What's the theme or most important development this month]

**Lo más destacado:**
• **[Topic 1]** — [One-line description with clear value]
• **[Topic 2]** — [One-line description]
• **[Topic 3]** — [One-line description]

**Del blog:**
• [Post title](link) — [One sentence summary]
• [Post title](link) — [One sentence summary]

**Fechas clave:**
• [Regulatory deadline or event, if applicable]

¿Tienes dudas sobre alguno de estos temas? Responde a este email y te ayudamos.

Un saludo,
[Name from PrivaLex team]
PrivaLex Partners
```

### Single-Topic Format

```
Subject: [Clear benefit or specific topic]

[Nombre],

[2-3 sentences: Why this matters RIGHT NOW — tie to a deadline, trend, or client question]

[Key points — bullets or short paragraphs, max 3-4]

[Single CTA: Book a call / Read the full guide / Reply to discuss]

Un saludo,
[Name]
```

---

## Step 5: Quality Check

Before presenting, verify:

- [ ] Subject line is specific and under 60 characters
- [ ] Body is under 200 words
- [ ] No banned words (from voice-and-tone)
- [ ] Each bullet has a benefit, not just a topic name
- [ ] One clear CTA
- [ ] Signed by a real person, not "El equipo de PrivaLex"
- [ ] Tone is personal and professional
- [ ] Regulatory information is current and accurate

---

## Step 6: Present Output

Present:

1. **Newsletter** (subject + body)
2. **Send timing recommendation** (best day/time for B2B compliance audience)
3. **A/B subject line** — provide an alternative subject line for testing
