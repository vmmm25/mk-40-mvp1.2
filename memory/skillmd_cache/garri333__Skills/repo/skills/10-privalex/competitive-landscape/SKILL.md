# Competitive Landscape

How to position PrivaLex against alternatives. Focus on genuine differentiation, not attacks.

## Positioning Philosophy

- **Never trash competitors directly** — Let the comparison speak for itself
- **Acknowledge strengths** — Credibility comes from honesty
- **Focus on our sweet spot** — Boutique, tech-native, multi-framework, EU-focused
- **Specifics over generics** — "205+ clients in 14 countries" not "trusted by many"

---

## Competitor 1: Vanta

**What they are:** Automated compliance SaaS platform (US-based)

**Their strengths:**
- Highly automated — integrates with cloud infrastructure for evidence collection
- Fast time-to-compliance for straightforward SOC 2 / ISO 27001
- Strong brand recognition in US startup ecosystem
- Good for companies with simple, standardized tech stacks

**Where PrivaLex wins:**
- **Human expertise vs. automation** — Complex regulatory interpretation requires judgment, not just checkboxes
- **EU regulatory depth** — RGPD, LOPDGDD, NIS2, DORA, ENS are not Vanta's strength
- **Customization** — SGSI adapted to your actual operations, not a generic template
- **Multi-framework** — One partner for ISO 27001 + ENS + RGPD + NIS2, not just one certification
- **Ongoing advisory** — Strategic compliance partner, not just a tool license
- **FUNDAE** — Training cost recovery not available through SaaS platforms

**Key message:**
Vanta automatiza compliance para startups con stacks estándar. PrivaLex te acompaña con criterio humano para retos regulatorios complejos en Europa.

**What NOT to say:**
- Don't say Vanta is "bad" — it serves a different need
- Don't compare pricing directly (different models: SaaS subscription vs. consulting engagement)

## Competitor 2: Across Legal

**What they are:** Boutique law firm specializing in startups, scaleups, and venture capital

**Their strengths:**
- Strong reputation in the Spanish startup ecosystem
- Deep legal expertise in tech, IP, and M&A
- Good relationships with VCs and accelerators

**Where PrivaLex wins:**
- **Security + compliance focus** — Across Legal is broader legal; PrivaLex is specialized in security and compliance
- **Certification capability** — We implement management systems and prepare for audits, not just legal advisory
- **Technical depth** — Our team understands cloud architecture, not just legal frameworks
- **Multi-framework certification** — ISO 27001, ENS, SOC 2, HIPAA — not just RGPD
- **Formación + FUNDAE** — Integrated training programs with cost recovery

**Key message:**
Across Legal is a great legal partner. PrivaLex is the compliance and certification specialist when you need to go beyond legal advice and actually implement.

**What NOT to say:**
- Don't position as competitors in legal services (M&A, IP, corporate)
- Don't compare legal expertise — different specialization

**Key message:**
Legal Army provides accessible digital legal services. PrivaLex delivers end-to-end compliance implementation and certification.

## Competitor 5: Vanta + OneTrust (Tool-Based Competitors)

**Their strengths:**
- Automation of compliance workflows
- Dashboard-based visibility
- Integration with tech infrastructure

**Where PrivaLex wins:**
- Tools require human configuration and interpretation
- PrivaLex can complement or replace tool-based approaches
- European regulatory nuance requires expert judgment
- Tools don't prepare you for auditor conversations
- Tools don't provide training or FUNDAE management

**Key message:**
Tools automate parts of compliance. PrivaLex provides the strategy, interpretation, and human judgment that tools can't.
