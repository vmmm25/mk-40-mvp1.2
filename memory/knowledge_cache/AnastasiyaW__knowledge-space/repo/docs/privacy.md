# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 2026

## What we collect

When you subscribe to updates, we store:

- Your email address
- Timestamp of subscription
- IP address (for consent verification)

We do not collect names, payment information, or any other personal data.

## Why we collect it

To send you occasional email updates when new articles are published on happyin.space.

## How we store it

Your data is stored on Cloudflare's infrastructure (D1 database), encrypted at rest.

## Your rights

- **Unsubscribe** at any time via the link in every email, or by contacting us
- **Request deletion** of your data by emailing us
- **Request export** of your data by emailing us

## Contact

For any privacy-related questions: [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/AnastasiyaW/knowledge-space/issues)

## Cookies

This site uses no tracking cookies. Cloudflare may set essential cookies for security purposes.
